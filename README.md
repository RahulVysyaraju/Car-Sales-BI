# Global Auto Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing global car sales performance, revenue trends, and geographical distribution for Global Auto Insights.
## Project Objective & Overview
This project features a comprehensive and interactive business intelligence dashboard created in Power BI to provide key stakeholders with a clear, 360-degree view of global car sales performance. The dashboard enables users to monitor real-time KPIs, track historical trends, and perform detailed analysis across different dealers, products, and geographies.
## Key Features and Insights Delivered
The dashboard is structured across multiple interactive pages (Exec. Dash, Map, Products, Dealers):

### 1. Executive Dashboard (Summary)
KPIs: Displays key metrics like $241.6M Total Revenue, 3,750 Total Units Sold, and $130.2K Average Revenue.

Performance Tracking: Features Monthly Revenue and Units Sold gauges with percentage change metrics compared to the previous period.

Top Performers: Ranks Top 5 Countries by Revenue and the Top 5 Car Brands (e.g., Toyota, Nissan) contributing to the $80.13M total of top brand sales.

### 2. Dealer & Geographical Analysis
Dealer Performance: Uses a scatter plot to correlate Dealer Performance (Volume vs. Value), helping identify high-performing branches.

Global Mapping : Displays Total Revenue by Countries, allowing users to filter geographical sales performance by Affordable, Expensive, and Medium price segments.

Trend Analysis: Shows Revenue Trends by Top 5 Countries over time (Jan 2017 â€“ Jan 2020) to track market changes.

### 3. Product Deep Dive
Best Seller: Highlights the performance of the Best Seller Car (Toyota Avalon), detailing its monthly revenue ($6.9M) and monthly units sold (97).

Sales Trends: Provides a time-series chart of Monthly Units Sold Trends to visualize seasonality and product momentum.
